Web

Yorestory

Yorestory gives you the opportunity to see where you fit into world history. After entering your birthday, an automatically-generated timeline appears, providing information and images to represent your life in history. The app makes use of the Wolfram Alpha and Wikipedia APIs to populate data to the timelines.

Q

Q lets you anonymously ask questions of other anonymous users, and see simple graphical representations of the results.

Comb

Comb is a mobile app for creating personalized maps and saving locations to view later or share with friends.

About

I'm passionate about art, design, and illustration, which is what I make with most of my time in Brooklyn, NY. I have a BFA in illustration and English from RISD, and a master's in art direction from BU. I've recently branched further into web development, and I'm excited to continue exploring. I would love to chat, so please give me a shout. Thanks for looking!
